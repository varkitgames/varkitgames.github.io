Privacy Policy for Mallow Mania

Effective date: June 30, 2026

VarKit Games operates the Mallow Mania mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you install or use Mallow Mania.

The current version of Mallow Mania does not include third-party advertising, does not require account registration and does not transmit gameplay analytics to an external analytics service.

1. Information We Collect

The current version of Mallow Mania does not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, phone number, postal address or date of birth.

The game may create and store gameplay-related information locally on your device, including:

  • current and completed level information
  • game progress and unlocked content
  • earned rewards, coins and boosters
  • task progress and task completion status
  • music, sound and vibration preferences
  • other game settings
  • local gameplay event logs used for debugging, testing and game improvement

2. How Information Is Used

Locally stored game information is used to:

  • save and restore your game progress
  • remember your settings and preferences
  • display completed levels and earned rewards
  • support task and booster functionality
  • diagnose technical problems during development and testing
  • improve game stability and gameplay behaviour

3. Local Storage

Game progress, settings and local gameplay logs are currently stored on your device.

VarKit Games does not currently upload these local gameplay logs to its own server or to a remote analytics provider.

4. Advertising

The current published version of Mallow Mania does not display third-party advertisements and does not currently include the Google AdMob SDK.

If advertising is added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when that version is released. Any required consent or privacy controls will also be implemented where applicable.

5. Third-Party Services

The current version does not use a third-party advertising SDK or remote analytics SDK.

Future versions may use third-party services for features such as advertising, crash reporting, cloud storage or analytics. If this happens, this policy will be updated to identify those services and explain their data handling.

6. Device Permissions

Mallow Mania does not currently request access to:

  • contacts
  • microphone
  • camera
  • precise location
  • photos or media files

Normal operating-system and network permissions may be added in future versions when required for legitimate game features or third-party services. This policy will be updated if those permissions affect user data.

7. Sharing of Information

VarKit Games does not currently sell, rent or share personal information collected through Mallow Mania.

Because the current version does not transmit local gameplay logs to a remote service, those logs are not currently shared with analytics or advertising providers.

8. Data Retention

Local game progress, preferences and logs may remain on your device until one of the following occurs:

  • you clear the application's data
  • you uninstall the application
  • the operating system removes the data
  • the game overwrites or rotates older local logs

The game limits the size of its local analytics log to reduce unnecessary storage growth.

9. Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored Mallow Mania data by clearing the application's storage through your device settings or by uninstalling the game.

The current version does not create an online user account and does not store user-specific game data on a VarKit Games server. Therefore, there is currently no remote account data to request for deletion.

10. Security

VarKit Games takes reasonable steps to limit unnecessary collection and storage of information.

However, no software or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should keep their devices and operating systems updated and protected.

11. Children's Privacy

Mallow Mania does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The current version does not include user accounts, direct messaging, social networking or behavioural advertising.

If the game is later directed to children or added to Google Play's Families programme, VarKit Games will review and update its data practices, advertising configuration and legal disclosures accordingly.

12. International Users

Mallow Mania may be available to users in different countries. Privacy rights and requirements may vary depending on your location.

Where applicable law provides additional privacy rights, you may contact VarKit Games using the email address below.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the game's features, data practices, permissions or third-party services change.

The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.
